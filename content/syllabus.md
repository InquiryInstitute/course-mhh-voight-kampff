## Policies

### Late Work
- 24-hour grace period, no penalty
- 24-72 hours: Revise pathway only (no Pass)
- Beyond 72 hours: Requires instructor approval

### Collaboration
- Discussion encouraged
- Code must be your own
- Cite all sources, including AI assistance
- Peer review is collaboration, not plagiarism

### AI Assistance Policy
You may use AI tools (ChatGPT, Copilot, etc.) with these requirements:
1. Document all AI assistance in your submission
2. You must understand and be able to explain any AI-generated code
3. The final analysis and argument must be your own
4. Using AI to complete assignments without understanding defeats the course's purpose

### Accessibility
- All readings available in accessible formats
- Symposium captions available on request
- Extended time accommodations honored
- Contact instructor for individual needs
